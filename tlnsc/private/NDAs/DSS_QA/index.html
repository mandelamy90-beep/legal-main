Disney's Storybook Studios – Quality Assurance Team - Non-Disclosure Agreement

Last updated: 29 June 2026
Jurisdiction: Ireland
This Non-Disclosure Agreement ("Agreement")

is entered into between:

The LoveNorSad Company ("Company")
and
Name: ___________________________

1. Purpose

The QA Team Member will receive access to confidential versions of the Company's Roblox experience currently known as Disney's Storybook Studios for the purpose of testing, identifying bugs, evaluating gameplay, and providing feedback.

Because this access may expose the QA Team Member to confidential information, both parties agree to the following terms.

2. Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes, but is not limited to:

  • Unreleased maps
  • Unreleased attractions
  • Unreleased rides
  • Scripts
  • Source code
  • Game assets
  • Models
  • Audio
  • Music
  • User Interfaces
  • Features under development
  • Bug reports
  • Internal documentation
  • Internal discussions
  • Development tools
  • Internal testing servers
  • Future plans
  • Releae schedules
  • Design documents
  • Artwork
  • Logos
  • Promotional material before publication
  • Screenshots
  • Videos
  • Photographs
  • Any other information marked as confidential or reasonably understood to be confidential.

    • Whether information is communicated verbally, digitally, visually, or in writing, it shall be considered confidential unless officially released by the Company.

3. Non-Disclosure Obligations

The QA Team Member agrees to:

  • Keep all Confidential Information strictly confidential.
  • Not share confidential information with any person unless expressly authorised in writing by the Company.
  • Not publish screenshots, videos, livestreams, recordings, or photographs of confidential builds.
  • Not discuss unreleased content publicly.
  • Not upload confidential materials to social media, Discord servers, YouTube, TikTok, Reddit, Roblox groups, or any other platform.
  • Not distribute copies of confidential game files.
  • Not permit another individual to access confidential builds using their account.

4. Permitted Use

The QA Team Member may use Confidential Information solely for:

  • Testing the experience
  • Finding bugs
  • Providing feedback
  • Reporting issues
  • Helping improve the project

No other use is permitted.

5. Intellectual Property

All game assets, scripts, artwork, code, documentation, concepts, and other materials remain the sole property of both The LoveNorSad Company and The Walt Disney Company.

Nothing in this Agreement transfers ownership of any intellectual property to the QA Team Member.

6. Return or Deletion of Materials

Upon request by the Company, or when the QA Team Member leaves the QA programme, the QA Team Member agrees to:

  • Delete confidential copies in their possession.
  • Delete downloaded screenshots unless authorised to retain them.
  • Remove local copies of testing builds where applicable.

7. Reporting Security Issues

If the QA Team Member discovers:

  • exploits
  • security vulnerabilities
  • leaked builds
  • unauthorised access

they agree to report them to the Company promptly and not disclose them publicly.

8. Exceptions

This Agreement does not apply to information that:

  • becomes publicly available through official Company release;
  • was already lawfully known to the QA Team Member before disclosure;
  • is independently developed without using Confidential Information; or
  • must be disclosed by law, provided the Company is notified where legally permitted.

9. No Employment Relationship

This Agreement does not create:

  • employment;
  • partnership;
  • agency;
  • joint venture; or
  • ownership rights.

The QA Team Member participates as an independent tester unless otherwise agreed in writing.

10. Term

This Agreement takes effect on the Effective Date and remains in force until:

  • the Confidential Information is publicly released by the Company; or
  • the Company provides written notice releasing the QA Team Member from these obligations.

The confidentiality obligations survive the QA Team Member leaving the QA programme.

11. Breach

If the QA Team Member breaches this Agreement, the Company may:

  • immediately revoke testing access;
  • remove the QA Team Member from internal teams;
  • request deletion of confidential materials;
  • seek any remedies available under applicable law.

12. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of Ireland. Disputes arising out of or in connection with this NDA shall be subject to the exclusive jurisdiction of the Irish courts unless otherwise agreed in writing.

13. Entire Agreement

This document constitutes the entire agreement relating to confidentiality between the Company and the QA Team Member and supersedes any previous verbal or written understandings concerning confidential information.
